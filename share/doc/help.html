Help for Sync Saved Windows

Supported websites

Due to the limitation of tabs.update provided by the WebExtension, the following URLs will not be saved:
  • chrome: URLs
  • javascript: URLs
  • data: URLs
  • file: URLs

Google drive

When using Google drive, please wait until the notification before closing the add-on window. This is due to the fact that it takes a bit longer to update to the Google drive storage than local storage.
